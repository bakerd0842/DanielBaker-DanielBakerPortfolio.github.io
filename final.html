Final Project

The Seal With a Horrific Degree of Amnesia

Description

In this final project, the player was to specify their color and name of a particular seal, which would be manipulatable through the “a” and “d” keys. Then, the player was to play 2 rounds, where the player must pursue and avoid the ball of their color preferences, respectively.
