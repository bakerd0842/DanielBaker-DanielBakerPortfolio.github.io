Karate

Reading

Reading is paramount to learning, and even reading fiction can enhance one's academic ability and knowledge of the world. However, though I often don't get around to it till the weekends, I often read independent of school as it can be quite fun. My favorite genres to read are non-fiction or historical fiction, although science fiction or dystopian future type books can be intriguing, though I regard them as often over-rated

Acting/Videography

It should be noted that I have taken no classes in these areas and thus my ability in doing either is lacklustre, but I love videography and acting. In the past, I used to have a Youtube channel where I would upload and edit random skits of me with my family or friends, although recently I have largely been unactive and my channel is thus stagnant. I love to act, and often did so on my aforementioned Youtube site. In fact, though I never took any acting classes, I received the lead role of the sixth grade play

Learning

If I had to select a favorite hobby, it would be learning. This is because as though you may get bored doing so, the more you know, the more you realise that you don't know. In addition, learning new things opens up so many new opportunities
